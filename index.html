The Tefem Campus Ambassador Program is now open!

This initiative by Tefem is aimed at recruiting students in tertiary institutions

To represent the Tefem brand and increase awareness of her programs in their Institution

How you will spend your time as a Tefem Campus Ambassador

Get students to register and join Tefem campus community.

Commit at least 6 months of their academic year to the ambassador program

Organising tech trainings and meetups in your campus

Be reliable and punctual Attend meetings and events organised by tefem

Campus Ambassador Program Requirements

Pursuing an undergraduate. postgraduate. PhD or any program spanning a minimum of 1 year

Passionate about technology and Impacting communities.

An active member or a number or student groups. clubs and associations on your campus

Currently enrolled in a tertiary Institution & have an active social media account

All universities, polytechnics, or college of education in Nigeria are eligible
